Guide & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I take a test?

    A: To take a test, navigate to the Test Library page and select the test you want to take.

  • Q: How do I view my test results?

    A: Your test results will be displayed on the dashboard page after you submit your test.

Guide

This guide will walk you through the process of using our platform.

  1. Step 1: Create an account and log in.
  2. Step 2: Navigate to the Test Library page and select a test.
  3. Step 3: Take the test and submit your answers.
  4. Step 4: View your test results on the dashboard page.
